Description

Yam and stew is a traditional dish in many West African countries, featuring boiled yam served with a rich, flavorful tomato-based stew. The stew can include various ingredients such as meat, fish, or vegetables, making it a hearty and satisfying meal.

Ingredients

  • 2 large yams, peeled and cut into chunks
  • 4 cups of water
  • 1 can of diced tomatoes
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 2 cloves of garlic, minced
  • 2 tablespoons of vegetable oil
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Optional: meat or fish for added protein

Steps

  1. In a pot, bring 4 cups of water to a boil. Add the yam chunks and cook until tender, about 20-25 minutes.
  2. In another pot, heat vegetable oil over medium heat. Saut√© chopped onions and minced garlic until fragrant.
  3. Add the diced tomatoes and cook for about 10 minutes, stirring occasionally.
  4. If using meat or fish, add it to the stew now and cook until fully cooked through.
  5. Season with salt and pepper to taste.
  6. Serve the boiled yam with the tomato stew on top or on the side.
