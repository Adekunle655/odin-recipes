Description

Rice and stew is a classic dish in many cultures, consisting of cooked rice served with a flavorful tomato-based stew. The stew can include various ingredients such as meat, fish, or vegetables, making it a versatile and satisfying meal.

Ingredients

  • 2 cups of rice
  • 4 cups of water
  • 1 can of diced tomatoes
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 2 cloves of garlic, minced
  • 2 tablespoons of vegetable oil
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Optional: meat or fish for added protein

Steps

  1. Rinse the rice under cold water until the water runs clear.
  2. In a pot, bring 4 cups of water to a boil. Add the rice and cook according to package instructions until tender.
  3. In another pot, heat vegetable oil over medium heat. Saut√© chopped onions and minced garlic until fragrant.
  4. Add the diced tomatoes and cook for about 10 minutes, stirring occasionally.
  5. If using meat or fish, add it to the stew now and cook until fully cooked through.
  6. Season with salt and pepper to taste.
  7. Serve the rice with the tomato stew on top or on the side.
